## Interpretation

Use these rows as authored title evidence:

- "Enable ranked this player #N for this title."
- "This is player-authored retrospective evidence, not fan consensus."
- "This is title-specific performance evidence, not all-time greatness."

Do not use these rows as:

- a community consensus score;
- a formal award/honor;
- an objective-stat model;
- a cross-title authored consensus.
