How this score is computed

Each enabled lane is percentile-normalized within the selected era, then weighted by your sliders.

ResumeEra-adjusted wins plus Champs rings times the extra value above 1x.
Individual SkillAdditive community-consensus score across selected titles.
LongevityNumber of selected-era titles with at least one major win.
PeakBest selected-era winning season plus best selected-era consensus peak.

Missing community-consensus rows are treated as neutral for the Skill lane in this mockup, not zero.
