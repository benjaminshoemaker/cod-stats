Methodology Panel

Expandable detail on the page or a methodology anchor.

Rank points: each title rank becomes max(0, (31 - rank) / 30) ^ 2.5.

Source weights: strong end-title surveys are 1.00; thinner or inclusion-frequency passes are discounted.

Boundaries: event wins are displayed as resume context but do not change the community score.
