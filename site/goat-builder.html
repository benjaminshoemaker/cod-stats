How this score is computed

Each enabled lane is converted to a 0-100 score, then weighted by your point allocation.

ResumeRing-weighted adjusted wins, scaled to the selected-era leader.
Individual SkillAdditive community-consensus score, scaled to the selected-era leader.
LongevityTitles with a recorded major placing, scaled to the selected-era leader.
PeakBest single-title 50/50 blend of title resume peak and same-title consensus score.
