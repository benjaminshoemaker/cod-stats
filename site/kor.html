Kills Over Replacement

Major-event kills per map above a title-and-mode replacement baseline. Respawn and S&D are separate because their kill environments are different.

Formula. KOR/map = player K/map − replacement K/map. Replacement is the 25th percentile K/map among qualified players in the same title and mode split. Read it as: +2.00 means about two more kills per map than a fringe qualified pro in that title and mode; 0 is replacement level, and the best all-time respawn rates sit around +5 to +6. Opponent placement columns are context only, not part of the score. Methodology →

Boards start at Black Ops 2. The wiki has no per-map kill data for earlier titles (CoD4, MW2, Black Ops), and Modern Warfare 3's on-record sample is too thin to qualify anyone.
